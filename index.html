Project: CoRaw

Working Title

CoRaw is a webapp designed to help users learn and practice conversational Irish using AI and meta-prompting.

Abstract

CoRaw is a webapp designed to help users learn and practice conversational Irish using AI and meta-prompting. The webapp will take the user through immersive realistic conversations that will translate to improving real world conversational skills in Irish. The idea is that CoRaw will give people who are learning the Irish language a chance to engage in realistic everyday conversations at their own pace with an AI model. Users will be presented with some questions when they first login to determine their level and topics they are interested in. CoRaw will then present them with their own learning dashboard.
